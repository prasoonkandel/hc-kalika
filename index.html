About HackclubButwal Kalika

Hackclub Butwal Kalika is a student-led coding club affiliated with the international Hackclub network. We're on a mission to encourage high school students studying in Kalika Manavgyan Secondary School, Butwal, Nepal.

Through workshops, events, and community activities, we empower students to build projects, learn new technologies, and collaborate with each other.

Join us to learn, build, and share your creative ideas in a supportive community!
